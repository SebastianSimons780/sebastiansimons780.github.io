The Lily Cottage

The Lily Cottage (named after the Arum Lilies surrounding it), sits over a beautiful natural spring and is a private apartment with all the amenities. It sleeps up to 3 people and has a small kitchen and an en suit bathroom. This option offers great privacy and flexibility.

Amenities

Private bathroom.
Fully kitted kitchenette.
Access to braai area, natural pool, table tennis, pool table and WiFi.
